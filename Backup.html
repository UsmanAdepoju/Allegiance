Backup & Disaster Recovery

Though most business owners understand the risks associated with major disasters, many don’t realize that most of the data loss is often the result of small localized problems. These are important considerations for any business owner, but for small and mid-sized businesses, these realities leave no doubt about the value of having a Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

  • “Real Time” Backup Solution - Utilizes “Incremental Forever” methodology to capture changes to your data every 15 minutes.
  • Server Virtualization Technology – Allows your servers to be restored and rebooted in less than 30 minutes.
  • Advanced Data Encryption – Ensures that your information remains safe, secure and compliant.
  • Built-in Archiving – Provides a more cost-effective option than tape.
  • 24/7 Monitoring and Management – Guarantees complete integrity of your data.
  • Complete Image Generation – Creates an exact digital duplicate of your server infrastructure
